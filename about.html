About Us


Encompassing over 130 stores and eateries, Pine City Mall is the ultimate shopping destination. Pine City Mall embraces the upper highway ethos, being a friendly, warm and welcoming neighbor. Delighting in celebrating the beauty and bounty of this special corner of South Africa, the 80,000sqm mall is located in the suburb of Pinefall in Pine City, Gauteng. Pine City centre is only a 30 minute drive from Pine City Mall, and there is easy access to both the N3 and M13.
