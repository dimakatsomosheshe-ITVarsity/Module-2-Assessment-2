BEAST

Fight for family.


A father and his two teenage daughters find themselves hunted by a massive rogue lion intent on proving that the savannah has but one apex predator.


Dr. Nate Daniels, a recently widowed husband, returns to South Africa, where he first met his wife, on a long-planned trip with his daughters, to a game reserve managed by Martin Battles, an old family friend and wildlife biologist. But what begins as a journey of healing jolts into a fearsome fight for survival when a lion, a survivor of blood-thirsty poachers who now sees all humans as the enemy, begins stalking them.



Age Restriction: 16

Genre: Thriller

Cast: Idris Elba, Iyana Halley, Leah Sava Jeffries, Sharlto Copley, Riley Keough, Melanie Jarnson, Damon Burtley, Amara Miller, Robby MacIsaac, Billy Gallagher
